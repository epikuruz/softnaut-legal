Who is it for?

Anyone who wants to be faster at mental arithmetic — students preparing for standardized tests, adults who want to sharpen a skill they once knew, or anyone who's curious how fast they can get.

  • K–12 students building foundational fluency
  • Adults brushing up on multiplication and division
  • Competitive math students training for speed

How it works

Mathonaut runs short, focused drills — typically 30 to 60 seconds. After each drill, it records which problems you got right, wrong, or slow, and adjusts what you see next. Practice every day and the hardest problems become easy ones.
