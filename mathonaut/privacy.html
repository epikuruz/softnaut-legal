Mathonaut — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 May 2026

Mathonaut is built so you don't have to give us anything. Here's what that means in practice.

What we collect

Nothing. Mathonaut does not have an account system, does not ask for your name, email, or phone number, and does not include any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDK.

What is stored on your device

Your training sessions, scores, statistics, achievements, settings, and subscription state are stored locally on your device using its built-in database (IndexedDB). This data never leaves your device and is not sent to us. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all of it.

What happens when you subscribe to Mathonaut Pro

Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We never see your payment details. Apple's privacy practices govern that transaction — see Apple's privacy policy.

To manage subscription state across app launches and devices signed in to the same Apple ID, we use a third-party service called RevenueCat. RevenueCat receives an anonymous purchase receipt from Apple and an app-generated anonymous identifier. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, Apple ID, or any other personal data — we don't have it to give. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for their practices.

If you redeem a promo or gift code, RevenueCat records the code and the anonymous identifier it was applied to so the entitlement can be honored.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell, share, or rent any data.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or location.
  • We do not require an internet connection to use the app after the first launch.

Children's privacy

Mathonaut is suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect any personal information from any user, the app complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) including provisions for minors, and equivalent regulations elsewhere.

Your rights

Because Mathonaut does not collect or store any personal information about you on our servers, there is nothing on our side for us to access, export, or delete on your behalf. To delete all locally stored data, uninstall the app from your device. To cancel a subscription, use your Apple ID subscription settings.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles data — for example, if we add an optional feature that requires data to be processed — we will update this page and revise the date at the top.
